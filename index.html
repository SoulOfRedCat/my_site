Who am I:
I'm a beginner pixelart artist, so I don't think I have a specific style or preference for what I create. I'd like to try a lot of different things, from small sprites to full-fledged art.

What I like:
Oddly enough, I love pixel graphics and games with pixel graphic like Stardew Valley or Terraria. I also enjoy electronic and instrumental music and I'm a big fan of cats.

What in future:
The first and most obvious is to create pixelart that gets better and better, so that everyone can find something enjoyable in my work.
The second is to create mods or texture packs and (or) mods for some games.
And the third, and most ambitious, is to create my own games. I'm not ready to talk about it yet, as it's a very complex and involved process.
